# Privacy Policy for Tadabbur

*Last updated: April 13, 2026*

## Overview

Tadabbur is a Quranic vocabulary learning app. It does not collect, store, or share personal information.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All learning progress — including known words, review schedule, and activity history — is stored locally on your device using a private database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

## Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification content is transmitted to any server.

## Bug Reports

If you submit a bug report via the in-app form, it is submitted to Google Forms. The report may include a description you write. [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) applies to that submission.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
